An “accessibility widget” won’t save you

Overlay tools (accessiBe, UserWay, AudioEye…) promise instant compliance. Courts disagree: the US FTC fined accessiBe $1,000,000 in 2025 for that exact claim, and a large share of accessibility lawsuits target sites that already run one. Real conformance means fixing the underlying code.
